Our Story

About Meat Republic Meru

A Meru institution built on fire, flavour, and family.

🔥

Born in Meru, Rooted in Tradition

Meat Republic Meru started with a simple idea: serve the freshest, most flavourful nyama choma in Meru Town. Located opposite Meru National Polytechnic, we have been fire-grilling premium goat, beef, pork, chicken and fish for the Meru community since 2016.

Our charcoal pits never sleep. Every morning, fresh meat is sourced directly from trusted local farmers and butcheries in the Mount Kenya region. By midday, the aroma of sizzling choma fills the street — and by evening, our tables are full of families, friends, students and workers who know exactly where to find the real thing.
